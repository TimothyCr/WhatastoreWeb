About WhataStore

We are on a mission to democratize e-commerce for merchants in emerging markets.

Our Story

WhataStore started with a simple observation: millions of small businesses run entirely on WhatsApp, but they lack the tools to scale. They manually copy-paste product photos, calculate totals on calculators, and struggle to track orders.

We built WhataStore to solve this.

By transforming WhatsApp into a powerful e-commerce platform, we enable merchants to automate their sales, manage inventory, and accept payments—all without asking their customers to download a new app.

Our Vision

We believe that anyone, anywhere, should be able to start and grow a business using the tools they already have in their pocket. We are building the future of conversational commerce.
